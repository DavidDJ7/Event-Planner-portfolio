Venue Guide

Top 20 wedding venues in Tamil Nadu for every budget

May 4, 2026 5 min read By Bharath K.

Finding the right venue is the single biggest decision in wedding planning. It affects your budget, guest experience, decor possibilities, and even the flow of the ceremony. Over the past 12 years, we've coordinated events at 100+ venues across Tamil Nadu. Here are our top 20, categorized by budget and city, with real pricing as of 2026.

Note: All prices below are venue rental only (food, decor, and photography extra). Prices vary by season — peak season (Nov-Feb) commands a 20-30% premium.

Insider tip: Temple premises are highly underrated for the ceremony portion. The ambiance is unmatched, the acoustics are perfect for vedic chants, and many temples now allow professional photography with permission. You save on decor too — temple architecture is the decor. Just book well in advance and secure written permission.

Factors to consider beyond price

  • Parking. A venue with inadequate parking creates a bad first impression. Ensure at least 1 slot per 10 guests.
  • AC vs outdoor. Tamil Nadu weddings are year-round. AC ballrooms are essential for summer (March-July). Outdoor venues work well for December-February evening events.
  • Kitchen capacity. If you're serving a sadya to 500+ guests, the kitchen must be able to handle the volume. Ask to see the kitchen during your venue visit.
  • Power backup. Non-negotiable. Ask about generator capacity and fuel backup duration.
  • Bridal room. A comfortable, clean, spacious room for the bride and family to prepare and rest. This matters more than most people think.
