Iyer vs Iyengar weddings: understanding the differences

Both Iyer and Iyengar weddings follow the Tamil Brahmin tradition, but the differences matter. Iyer weddings typically have the *Kashi Yatra* (where the groom pretends to leave for Kashi) and the bride's brother brings him back. Iyengar weddings include the *Saptapadi* (seven steps) around a sacred fire but omit the *Kashi Yatra*. The *Muhurtham* timing also differs — Iyers follow the *Rahu Kalam* avoidance while Iyengars follow *Vaishnava* traditions. Food too: Iyer weddings traditionally serve a fully vegetarian *sadya* on banana leaves, while some Iyengar communities include specific temple-style prasadams.

Top 20 wedding venues in Tamil Nadu for every budget

From grand 5-star ballrooms to intimate temple *mandapams*, Tamil Nadu offers incredible venue diversity. Chennai's ITC Grand Chola and Leela Palace lead the luxury segment (₹15L+). For mid-range budgets, Coimbatore's Kodai Road resorts and Madurai's heritage palaces offer stunning backdrops at ₹5–10L. Budget-friendly options include Trichy's Sangam Halls and dedicated Kalyana Mandapams starting at ₹1L. Don't overlook temple premises for religious ceremonies — many allow event setups with prior permission. We've personally coordinated events at 100+ venues across TN and maintain a venue shortlist with real pricing — no markups.
