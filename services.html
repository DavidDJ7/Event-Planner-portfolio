Engagement models

Three ways to work with us

Choose the level of involvement that fits your family — from full-service to consulting.

01

Complete

We handle everything. You just show up and enjoy. 15-30 person on-ground team, dedicated Event Parent, end-to-end logistics.

02

Semi-Managed

You manage select aspects (e.g. catering). We handle the rest — decor, vendors, sound, photography, and coordination.

03

Consulting

Expert guidance for families who want to plan independently. Hourly or fixed-fee sessions with a senior planner.

Weddings & Receptions

  • Full wedding design & planning
  • Mandapam & stage decoration

  • Ritual coordination (Muhurtham)

  • Guest management & RSVP

Corporate & Business Events

Conferences, product launches, award ceremonies, and team gatherings — executed with professionalism and discretion.

  • Conference & seminar management
  • Product launches & press meets
  • Award ceremonies & galas
  • Team outings & offsites
  • Stage, AV, and lighting
  • Catering & guest hospitality

Catering & Cuisine

25+ partner caterers across Tamil Nadu. From traditional sadya to live food stations — every palate covered.

  • Traditional Tamil sadya (banana leaf)

  • Live food stations
  • Menu tasting sessions
  • Dietary-specific menus (veg, Jain, keto)

Entertainment & Artists
