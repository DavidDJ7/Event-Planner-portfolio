Our story

We started with a simple belief.

That families should enjoy their own celebrations — not manage them. Since 2014, that belief has driven everything we do.

The beginning

One wedding. ₹50,000. Five friends.

In 2013, our founder watched the mother of the bride running around at 4 AM — checking food, managing vendors, directing photographers. By the time the ceremony started, she was exhausted. She missed her daughter's wedding.

That shouldn't happen. So we built a company to make sure it never does. Today, we're 60+ people across four cities, and every client gets one dedicated team member who handles everything — so they can just be present.

850+ events. Zero cancellations.

We've never failed to deliver an event. Rain, pandemic, or last-minute chaos — we show up.

How we grew

Our timeline

  • 2013

    The spark: a family wedding where the mother missed everything. The founder decided to change how events work.

  • 2014

    Launched with ₹50,000. First office: a 10×10 room in Trichy. Team: five college friends.

  • 2017

    Incorporated. Chennai office opened. First ₹1Cr revenue year. Team of 15.

  • 2020

    COVID hit. 25 weddings canceled. Survived on micro-weddings. Built our online presence.

  • 2022

    Post-COVID boom — 200+ events. Madurai office. In-house photo/video team launched.

  • 2023

    Won "Best Wedding Planner — Tamil Nadu." NRI wedding division launched.

  • 2025

    600+ events. 60 team members. Revenue crosses ₹5Cr. Franchise model in planning.

  • 2026

    850+ events. Launching tech upgrades, diaspora services. The story continues.
